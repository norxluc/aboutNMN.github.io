About Me

Hi there! I’m a grad student interested in research exploring the neuromechanics of movement in the context of neurorehabilitation and motor recovery.

I have prior experience of motion analysis in clinical biomechanics, human performance and oculomotor dysfunction labs. I hope to learn more about bridging neuroscience and rehabilitation to enhance motor recovery in clinical populations.

Research Interests

  • Sensorimotor neural plasticity
  • Locomotor adaptation and gait rehabilitation
  • Neural stimulation recording and analysis
  • Signal coherence and connectivity
